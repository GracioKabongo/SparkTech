Template Installation - FTP Upload

1. Open up your FTP manager and connect to your hosting.
2. Browse to required directory (Normally public_html)
3. Upload the files inside urho folder.

<script>
    appPreloader();
    appNavigation();
    appLogoDark();

In the <script> tag, it calls the function needed for the subpage.

Each single subpage has a different call function, depending on the subpage's needs.

Logo

<!-- Logo -->
<a href="index.html" class="nav-brand">
    <img src="images/logo.svg" alt="alternative text" class="logo">

    <!-- If you don't have a logo, you can use the text version of the brand name. Use the tag below and remove the img tag above. -->
    <!-- <span>Company Name.</span> -->
</a>

To change the Urho logo, replace the picture logo.svg in the images folder.

If you also have a dark version of the logo, replace it with logo-dark.svg in the images folder.

You don't need to add extra html tag, java script will do it for you.


If you don't have the dark version of the Logo, remove call function appLogoDark(); from the <script> tag, at the bottom of the <body> tag.


<script>
    appLogoDark();
</script>

Contact Form

Open mail.php file from the root template folder with any text editor and change $recepient variable to your own e-mail address.

<?php

// Your email address
$recepient = "urho@contact.com";

$name = $_POST["name"];
$from = $_POST["email"];
$subject = $_POST["subject"];
$message = $_POST["message"];

$txt = "Email from: " . $name . "\r\n" . "Email address: " . $from . "\r\n" . "\r\n" . "Message: " . "\r\n" . $message . "\r\n" . "\r\n" . "--------" . "\r\n" . "This email was sent from your site's contact form.";

$headers = "From: " . $from . "\r\n";
$headers .= "Reply-To: " . $from . "\r\n";

mail($recepient, $subject, $txt, $headers);

?>
