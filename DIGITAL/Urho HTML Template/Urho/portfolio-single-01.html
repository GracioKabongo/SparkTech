Design

Business Card for Filmmaker

Designer, elegant and modernist style of graphics made for the design of a business card for a graphic artist and illustrator, but above all a passionate filmmaker.

Description of The Task

The order consisted in creating a completely new business card design for a filmmaker who wanted to keep his current branding of his company so that it would always be recognizable.

The composition of the graphics for the business card was to be delicate, but also infantile, while maintaining professionalism.

  • Use of current branding
  • Create a new creation with a vision
  • Preparation for printing

Technologies Used

Our goal is to create exceptional graphics for a demanding customer, which is why we always use only the most professional software.

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Canon 5D mark III

Summary of The Implementation

Delicate and decorative graphic paper designed for printing invitations and business cards has proved to be a great solution for this graphic project.
