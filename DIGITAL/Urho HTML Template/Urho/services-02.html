Web Design

Web design is nothing more than creating the appearance of websites. The process is roughly the layout, design, and commissioning of your website. It is an interdisciplinary field that requires combining aesthetic sense, creative thinking and technical skills.

Web design has appeared quite recently. The first websites that were created in the early 90s were very simple. They consisted almost entirely of text. Due to the advancement of technology, photos and animations quickly appeared on them.

It was then that there was a need to professionally design websites in such a way that they were user-friendly and loaded as quickly as possible.

The real breakthrough came with the spread of smartphones. For some time now, the majority of website visits have come from mobile devices, and the phrase "next year will be the year of mobile" is so obvious that it has become the subject of jokes.
